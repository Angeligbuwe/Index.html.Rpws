About

Jollof Rice

It is a popular dish in many West African countries, including Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, and Gambia.
Each country has its own variation of the dish, but the basic ingredients remain the same.

Instructions on how to cook Jollof Rice

  1. Boil chicken with onion, garlic salt, seasoning cube, thyme and curry.
  2. While the meat is boiling, blend tomatoes, bell peppers, onion until smooth.
  3. Transfer pepper mix to a wide pot and boil for about (10-15 minutes)
  4. When meat is cooked, separate from stock, air-fry until golden brown
  5. Wash rice multiple times until water runs clear and set aside.
  6. In a large wide pot, heat up vegetables oil or oil of choice.
  7. Fry chopped onion until onion is translucent and fragrant.
  8. Add tomato paste and fry tomato paste for about 15 minutes.
  9. Boiled down pepper mix,season with salt, curry, and thyme.
  10. Fry for an additional 10 minutes. Add meat stock or water. Stir.
  11. Add washed rice, cover with foil and allow to cook for about 20 minutes.
  12. After 20 minutes your rice should be ready.
  13. Serve with chicken, fried plantain and salad.
  14. Enjoy!
